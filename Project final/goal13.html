What is Goal 13?

It aims to take urgent action to combat climate change and its impacts.

Goal 13 aims to take action against human caused climate change. Climate change is a real and undeniable threat.The effects will be catastrophic unless we act now. Through commiting to take action against climate change, we can make the necessary changes to protect the planet.

Targets of Goal 13

IMPLEMENT THE UN FRAMEWORK CONVENTION ON CLIMATE CHANGE

For countries to have a combined $100 billion used to help delevloping countires take climate action.

PROMOTE MECHANISMS TO RAISE CAPACITY FOR PLANNING AND MANAGEMENT

To help developing regions with their capacity to plan and take action against climate change.

STRENGTHEN RESILIENCE AND ADAPTIVE CAPACITY TO CLIMATE RELATED DISASTERS

To better prepare and prevent natrual disasters caused by climate change.

INTEGRATE CLIMATE CHANGE MEASURES INTO POLICIES AND PLANNING

Integrate climate change measures into national policies, strategies and planning.

BUILD KNOWLEDGE AND CAPACITY TO MEET CLIMATE CHANGE

Educate and raise awareness of the issue of climate change, and how to reduce its impacts.
